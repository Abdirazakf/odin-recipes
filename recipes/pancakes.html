Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

As convenient as a store bought mix can be, every home cook should have a good buttermilk pancake recipe in their arsenal. Here's a classic buttermilk pancake recipe to get light, fluffy, moist, and rich buttermilk pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour: All-purpose flour contains gluten, which gives the buttermilk pancakes structure and pleasant chewiness.
  • Sugar: Just three tablespoons offwhite sugar should be enough to lend subtle sweetness. Remember, you'll be adding a lot more sugar if you top the pancakes with syrup or molasses!
  • Leaveners: Baking powder and baking soda act as leaveners (a substance that releases gas when mixed with other ingredients, causing the batter to rise to fluffy perfection).
  • Salt: You definitely don't want to skip adding a small amount of salt â€” the buttermilk pancakes will taste bland without it.
  • Buttermilk: Of course, you'll need buttermilk for buttermilk pancakes! This recipe calls for three whole cups.
  • Milk: Whole milk helps create the perfect batter consistency and cuts some of the buttermilk's acidity.
  • Eggs: Three whole eggs add structure and rich flavor to the buttermilk pancakes
  • Butter: Melted butter lends flavor and keeps the pancakes nice and moist.
