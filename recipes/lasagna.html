World's Best Lasagna

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 2 hrs 30 mins Additional Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 3 hrs 15 mins Servings: 12

Description

This is a top-performing lasagna recipe that has racked up more than 7 million views. While making a lasagna can be quite time consuming, the results are well worth the wait and your family will also agree.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This meat lovers lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and Seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: You can use store bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce: In a large pot or Dutch oven, cook the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is well browned. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and half the fresh parsley. Simmer for at least one hour, stirring occasionally, to develop flavor.
  2. Cook the noodles: In a large pot of lightly salted boiling water, cook the lasagna noodles for about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain them and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking. Set them aside so they don't stick.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture: In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with the egg, remaining parsley, and a pinch of salt. Mix until it's well blended and set aside.
  4. Layer the lasagna: Spread 1½ cups of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over the sauce, then spread on half the ricotta mixture. Top with about a third of the mozzarella, followed by 1½ cups of sauce and a sprinkle of Parmesan. Repeat the layers. Finish with the remaining noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake: Preheat the oven to 375°F. Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil, being careful not to let the foil touch the cheese. Bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving: Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for about 15 minutes before cutting into it. This helps the layers set and makes for easier slicing.
