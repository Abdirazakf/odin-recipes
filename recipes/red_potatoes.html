Roasted New Red Potatoes

Description

Here's a recipe for Roasted red potatoes at their best, a plain and simple recipe for red potatoes tossed in olive oil with added salt and pepper then roasted to perfection.

  • Red Potatoes: 3 pounds of small red potatoes (halved)
  • Olive Oil: 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Seasonings: 1 teaspoon of salt and freshly ground black pepper
